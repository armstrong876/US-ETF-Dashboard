Calculation Methodology

How we calculate Momentum

The Momentum Score is a composite value calculated using weighted trailing returns:

  • 1 Month Return: 20% weight
  • 3 Month Return: 30% weight
  • 6 Month Return: 30% weight
  • 12 Month Return: 20% weight
Score = (1M × 0.2) + (3M × 0.3) + (6M × 0.3) + (12M × 0.2)

This ensures we capture both short-term performance and long-term established trends while giving higher importance to the 3-6 month window.

Momentum Signal Thresholds

  • 🟢 Strong: Score ≥ 15 (Strong upward trend)
  • 🟡 Neutral: Score between 5 and 15 (Consolidating)
  • 🔴 Weak: Score < 5 (Downward trend / underperforming)

Momentum Buy/Sell Signals

Calculated using trailing momentum: 🟢 Strong Signal (Score ≥ 15) | 🟡 Neutral (Score 5 to 15) | 🔴 Weak (Score < 5)
